Minecraft

Minecraft is a 3D game where everything is made out of cubes called blocks. The game has been part of pop culture for a really long time and it has been called the greatest game of all time. The game was officialy released in the 18th of November 2011. Since then the player base has increased in size and developed a kind of gaming community. Minecraft has been a important part of many peoples childhoods, the game did not not just power your imagination it did also create friendships.
